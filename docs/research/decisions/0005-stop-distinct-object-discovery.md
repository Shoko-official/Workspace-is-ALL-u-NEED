## Context

Decision 0004 required a new independently motivated `DISTINCT_CANDIDATE` before any implementation or article work could resume. The repository owner also required that the eventual article direct future LLM systems toward managed persistent state and adaptive computation without merely rewriting earlier work.

M1c therefore generated sixteen technical objects from three independent lanes: revision and replay formalization, shared-state systems, and longitudinal memory dynamics. Each object had to state an observable or mechanism, a decisive falsifier, a negative regime, a strongest prior-art composite, a generic-theory reduction, and a minimum discrimination burden.

## Findings

### Revision, repair, and compaction

Exact revision replay and dependency closure reduce to dynamic dependence graphs and change propagation. Adaptive Functional Programming records actual data and control dependencies, propagates changed inputs, re-executes affected reads, and proves correspondence to complete re-evaluation under its safety assumptions.

Causal Agent Replay and CausalFlow already add the LLM-agent-specific interventional layer: stochastic forward replay, outcome distributions, confidence intervals, dependency-annotated traces, localized repairs, and interaction attribution. They do not solve the proposed global minimum-repair or minimal-closure problems; those residual objectives remain generic optimization or causal-sufficiency extensions without a supplied distinct estimator or theorem.

The rate-distortion treatment formally supplies the compaction objective, lower bounds, query-unknown irreversible loss, and a repeated-compaction prediction. It proposes calibrated confidence, loss attribution, abstention, re-expansion, fetch, and escalation as a research agenda rather than reporting or proving the proposed certificate. The residual answer-or-reject object reduces to lossy coding plus provenance or approximate membership and selective rejection; no new certificate theorem is supplied.

### Shared state, concurrency, and migration

Cordon supplies task-level semantic transactions, result lineage, shadow state, an effect outbox, recovery metadata, validation, commit, rollback, and audit. CoAgent supplies a fixed serial order, speculative writes, LLM-mediated conflict re-judgment and repair, saga inverses, and serializable quiescence. The verified anomaly hierarchy formalizes stale generation, phantom tools, causal cascades, and effect reordering.

Adding a stochastic decision-distance predicate does not escape semantic serializability and approximate probabilistic trace equivalence. Decision confluence is exactly application-invariant confluence; Theorem 1 of the cited I-confluence work gives the necessary and sufficient condition for valid coordination-free execution.

Rosetta Memory directly studies memory written by one LLM and read by another, with writer and reader operators and unseen-model replacement. AFTER evaluates versioned procedural memory across model backbones. Decision-preserving migration adds a generic non-inferiority contract but no new invariant.

Skill Drift Is Contract Violation is an exact collision for contract-carrying procedural memory under changing services, packages, APIs, schemas, and configurations. It extracts executable role-bearing environment contracts, validates known and live conditions, reports false-alarm and recall evidence, and uses violations to localize repair.

### Longitudinal dynamics and evaluation

A-MemGuard describes and measures self-reinforcing memory error cycles across rounds. Longitudinal Safety evaluates fixed read-only probes over accumulated memory snapshots and randomizes order to separate content from encounter sequence. The rate-distortion record predicts repeated-compaction amplification. None defines the proposed reproduction number or divergence exponent; those quantities are ordinary branching and finite-time stability summaries applied to observed phenomena, without a distinct estimator or mechanism.

The revision-hysteresis residual is also composite rather than direct. Ziegler drives an adaptive artificial agent through a continuous target change and reversal without reset, measures different control burden at the same target, and defines a signed hysteresis-loop area. Hyperproperties supplies the two-trace observational and probabilistic non-interference form; STALE, A-TMA, temporal data, and provenance supply the LLM stale-state and current-view ingredients. No examined record reports the exact LLM `A -> B -> A` protocol, but changing the application domain does not create a new hysteresis phenomenon, estimator, or invariant.

The final residual compared reset-per-episode rankings with persistent-stream rankings. Artifact Ecology defines reset, persistent, governed-persistent, and held-out inheritance conditions. ShiftBench already shows that longitudinal recovery evaluation can reverse memory-policy rankings and change system selection. MemDelta shows that controlled model and embedding factors can reverse memory-system conclusions and recommends rank-order stability checks. Bojinov and Shephard provide path-indexed potential outcomes and causal contrasts. EvoMemBench and Memory-R2 separately operationalize cross-episode state and fair comparisons from a shared memory state.

No examined document reports the exact inversion between reset-per-episode and persistent-stream rankings. Artifact Ecology anticipates the inheritance protocol; ShiftBench anticipates inversions of selection under longitudinal evaluation; MemDelta anticipates reversals of conclusion under controlled variation. The residual is therefore `ANTICIPATED_BY_COMPOSITE`, not `ANTICIPATED_DIRECTLY`. An observed inversion would extend these results to the inheritance axis without establishing a new estimator, mechanism, invariant, or general phenomenon.

### Search limitation

The search was targeted, current to 15 July 2026, and non-exhaustive. M1c makes no claim that all relevant literature was found. The decision uses direct positive collisions, explicit strongest-composite anticipations, generic reductions, and the candidate specifications' failure to supply a distinct technical object; no candidate is rejected from search silence.

## Decision

Return global `STOP` for the sixteen-object M1c candidate set.

- Zero candidates are `DISTINCT_CANDIDATE`.
- Revision hysteresis and reset-to-stream ranking reversal are `ANTICIPATED_BY_COMPOSITE`, not `ANTICIPATED_DIRECTLY`: their exact LLM application cells remain unreported, while their protocols, phenomenon classes, and estimators are reconstructible from positive primary records.
- No model, benchmark, implementation, experiment, training run, paid compute, or manuscript is authorized.
- The M1c negative audit is not the requested direction-setting article.

The owner's long-term objective remains valid as a research objective, but this candidate set does not satisfy it. Another reopening must begin with a genuinely new technical object, not with a narrower label for any M1c candidate.

## Anti-rewrite determination

The following are explicitly insufficient:

- presenting semantic transactions, serializable repair, dependency closure, causal replay, or compaction confidence as a new managed-state architecture;
- renaming a dynamic dependence graph, I-confluence, provenance recall, reject option, branching threshold, stability exponent, or carryover contrast with agent-memory terminology;
- turning API-drift contracts or cross-model procedural transfer into a new memory ABI without a new guarantee;
- instantiating the known hysteresis and two-trace objects with an LLM `A -> B -> A` history and calling the application cell a new phenomenon;
- running the reset-versus-stream comparison and treating any score difference or rank flip as proof of a new technical object; or
- publishing this negative gate as the requested article.

## Alternatives considered

- `GO` was rejected because no object survived direct anticipation, strongest-composite anticipation, and generic reduction.
- `PIVOT` to revision hysteresis or reset-versus-stream ranking reversal was rejected because exact application novelty is insufficient under the anti-assembly threshold. The positive composites already supply the protocol ingredients, phenomenon classes, and ordinary estimators; neither candidate supplies a predictive law, mechanism, invariant, or causal estimator that escapes them.
- Running a minimum experiment before deciding was rejected because empirical execution cannot repair a failed originality gate.
- A survey or position paper was rejected by the owner's anti-rewrite requirement and Decision 0004.
