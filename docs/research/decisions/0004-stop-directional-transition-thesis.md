# Decision 0004: Stop the managed-state transition thesis

Date: `2026-07-14`
Status: `ACCEPTED`
Issue: [#5](https://github.com/Shoko-official/Workspace-is-ALL-u-NEED/issues/5)
Milestone: `M1b - Managed-state transition thesis novelty gate`
Supersedes: the directional-pivot authorization in [Decision 0003](0003-m1-stop-budgeted-workspace-route.md); all earlier architecture and empirical `STOP` decisions remain in force

## Context

Decision 0003 stopped the architecture, benchmark, and resource-accounting article routes. It retained one separately gated objective requested by the repository owner: give future LLM research a concrete direction for moving from prompt and context accumulation toward managed active state, governed persistent memory, and adaptive computation.

The owner also required that the article not be a rewrite of earlier work. Issue #5 therefore treated the direction as an unsupported candidate rather than as permission to publish a synthesis. It required operational objects, falsifiable predictions, negative regimes, non-isomorphic implementation classes, minimum discriminating designs, and reductions against both the strongest prior-art composite and generic systems theory.

## Candidate audited

M1b tested whether pre-outcome workload descriptors `Omega`, guarantee contracts `G`, and a vector resource envelope `B` could support an implementation-neutral rule

`T(Omega,G,B,E) -> (state_obligation, compute_obligation, decision_status)`

with an adequacy relation `A` that distinguishes semantic violation, resource violation, incomparability, and abstention. The intended output was a predictive transition framework, not an architecture label.

The audit registered five predictions:

1. a context-only negative regime;
2. a current-version and provenance feasibility boundary;
3. a fixed-versus-adaptive compute boundary;
4. a state-validity-before-compute interaction; and
5. abstention under insufficient coverage or identifiability.

## Findings

### Direct direction collision

[State-Aware Runtime](https://doi.org/10.33774/coe-2026-vt9t2-v2) is a direct framework-and-research-agenda collision. It separates generation from canonical state and places validation, commit, rollback, recovery, permissions, and audit in an explicit runtime-governance layer. Its stated direction is that long-horizon reliability should depend less on prompt accumulation and more on managed state.

[PLACEMEM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.04089v1) independently proposes a compute-aware and correction-aware memory plane with versioned validity, provenance, invalidation, reusable runtime state, KV-aware routing, and a roadmap. Together these records anticipate the broad transition direction and its strongest state-plus-compute composite.

The intended article could restate these systems under a unified vocabulary, but it would not produce a new observable decision, contract predicate, or falsifier.

### Contract and resource reduction

The candidate objects are reconstructible without LLM-specific theory:

- assume-guarantee contracts and QoS contracts supply implementation-neutral environments, obligations, tolerances, refinement, and composition;
- ClawVM, TOKI, Verifiable Memory Governance, and State-Aware Runtime supply typed fidelity, valid-time and transaction-time behavior, provenance, authorization, scoped retrieval, rollback, recovery, forgetting, validation, and audit;
- dominant-resource fairness, working-set theory, and stateful-system characterization supply vector resources, locality, lifecycle charges, feasibility, and Pareto comparisons;
- temporal database theory and W3C PROV supply validity, generation, use, invalidation, and ordering constraints; and
- reject-option theory supplies explicit abstention under uncertainty.

Writing these objects as `Omega`, `G`, `B`, `A`, and `T` introduces notation but no new invariant, lower bound, guarantee, or LLM-specific separation.

### State and compute boundaries

The memory-use regimes are also anticipated. When Should Memory Stay Silent separates storage consent, relevance, and warrant for current use. PersistBench, Shared Selective Persistent Memory, STALE, and A-TMA exhibit harmful persistence, stale-state failure, selective-memory benefit, and distinct bank, retrieval, and answering failures.

The compute boundary reduces to value of computation and cost-quality routing. Rational Metareasoning buys intermediate reasoning only when expected utility exceeds cost. X-Router separates evidence necessity from reasoning necessity and selects among four pipelines. Existing adaptive-compute and test-time-allocation work already covers fixed and adaptive stopping regimes.

### Narrowest residual

The narrowest residual asks whether validating governing state before allocating extra reasoning dominates a difficulty-only router when state is stale, missing, or contradictory. The proposed M1B-D04 factorial can test that interaction, but the residual does not survive the novelty gate.

STALE isolates current-state adjudication. A-TMA separates state-bank, retrieval, and answer failures across old, current, and transition facts. PLACEMEM couples validity, correction, runtime-state reuse, invalidation, and compute-aware routing. PersistBench shows that additional reasoning changes persistent-memory harms inconsistently across model families. X-Router and value-of-computation theory already separate evidence acquisition from further reasoning.

No exact calibrated cross-family coefficient was identified in the read source set, but no such estimator, coefficient, theorem, or margin is specified by the candidate either. An unreported factorial is an open measurement, not a distinct scientific object. Its disposition is `UNSUPPORTED_NOT_DISTINCT`.

### Falsifiability and feasibility

The five candidate predictions are falsifiable. Eight counterexample regimes and five non-circular minimum designs were recorded. Their combined lower bound is 131,200 query or policy executions before tuning, broader replication, lifecycle transactions, or independent reproduction. Model, API, energy, GPU-time, and storage costs remain `NON DISPONIBLE` because eligible model families and deployment modes were never frozen.

Falsifiability does not establish originality. No experiment is authorized because every prediction is anticipated, tautological under its assumptions, generic, or unspecified rather than distinct.

### Search sufficiency and limitation

The retained search log contains twelve registered broad query families, four narrow state-validity-by-compute queries, and fifteen exact identifier or canonical-record resolutions. Three independent audits recorded direct stateful-system collisions, generic contract reduction, and regime-boundary reduction.

The search is targeted and family-saturated, not exhaustive. It does not justify an absence-of-prior-art statement. This `STOP` instead rests on positive anticipation and exact reconstruction. Search silence around the precise M1B-D04 factorial is not used as novelty evidence.

## Decision

Return global `STOP` for the M1b direction-setting route.

- The broad future-facing direction is directly anticipated by State-Aware Runtime and PLACEMEM.
- `Omega`, `G`, `B`, `A`, and the qualitative `T` shell reduce to known stateful systems, contracts, resource allocation, temporal data, provenance, metareasoning, and reject decisions.
- Every registered prediction is anticipated, generic, tautological under declared information constraints, or unsupported without a distinct object.
- The validity-before-compute residual remains testable but is `UNSUPPORTED_NOT_DISTINCT`.
- Zero `DISTINCT_CANDIDATE` objects survive.
- The direction-setting article is `NOT_ELIGIBLE`.

Do not draft a manuscript, implement the stopped framework, execute its benchmark designs, train a model, or spend paid compute under issue #5.

## Anti-rewrite determination

The following do not satisfy the owner's requirement:

- renaming prompt-to-state runtime governance as a new transition theory;
- collecting memory validity, provenance, rollback, resource budgets, adaptive compute, and abstention into one diagram or notation;
- presenting known context-only, harmful-memory, selective-memory, or adaptive-compute regimes as new predictions;
- treating the absence of one exact factorial, estimator, or coefficient in a targeted search as a novelty result;
- weakening the failed thesis into a survey, manifesto, maturity model, migration roadmap, or research agenda; or
- publishing the M1b negative audit itself as the requested original article.

The managed-state direction may be useful engineering advice. The gate rejects it as this project's original scientific contribution, not as an engineering practice.

## Alternatives considered

- `GO` was rejected because no distinct technical object and no distinct prediction survived.
- A narrow `PIVOT` to validity-before-compute was rejected because its conceptual content is reconstructible and its missing quantitative rule is unspecified.
- Running M1B-D04 before deciding was rejected because a measurement cannot repair a failed originality gate when the candidate does not state a new estimator, law, or mechanism.
- A direction-setting survey or position paper was rejected because the owner explicitly prohibited a rewrite of earlier work.
- Retaining another architecture or benchmark pivot was rejected by Decisions 0002 and 0003.
